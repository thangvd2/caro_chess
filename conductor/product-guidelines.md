# Product Guidelines: Caro Chess (C·ªù Caro)

## Tone & Voice
- **Primary Tone:** Energetic & Exciting.
- **Communication Style:** The interface should use punchy, enthusiastic language to build hype and momentum. Avoid dry, purely technical descriptions in favor of engaging text (e.g., "VICTORY!" instead of "Game Over - You Win").

## Visual Identity
- **Typography & Colors:** Use bold, striking typography and a vibrant, high-contrast color palette to create a visually stimulating environment.
- **Animations:** Implement dynamic animations for key actions like piece placement, winning combinations, and menu transitions to keep the interface feeling alive.
- **Branding:** Maintain a consistent brand identity, including a recognizable logo and potentially a mascot, across Web, Desktop, and Mobile platforms.

## Feedback & Notifications
- **Visual Feedback:** Use impactful effects such as particle explosions or screen shakes to reward winning moves and significant plays.
- **Audio Cues:** Employ enthusiastic and satisfying sound effects for game interactions (e.g., "crunchy" placement sounds, celebratory win fanfares).
- **Messaging:** Ensure game status updates are clear, direct, and exciting (e.g., "Your Turn!", "EPIC WIN!").

## User Interface (UI/UX)
- **Layout Strategy:** Adopt a **Mobile-First & Responsive** design philosophy. The core interface should be optimized for touch interactions and scale elegantly to larger desktop screens.
- **Navigation:** Prioritize intuitive, easy-to-reach controls suitable for both handheld devices and mouse input.

## Localization & Language
- **Universal Design:** Heavily utilize icons and emojis to convey meaning, reducing the reliance on text and aligning with the high-energy, modern tone.
- **Language Support:** Provide full translation support, starting with **Vietnamese and English**, to cater to the core audience while preparing for broader reach.
